About Me

Steve is a Data Engineering Team Lead for Capital One with 5 years of experience. Before working in tech Steve spent a decade working in urban education as a physics teacher and then district administrator who specialized in analyzing student performance data. When not at work he enjoys sailing, rock climbing, amateur astronomy, camping, and cooking for his family. He lives in Milwaukee with his wife, daughters, dog, two cats and a leopard gecko.
